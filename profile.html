Toshiya Namikawa
Research Interests
I am broadly interested in intersection between theoretical and observational cosmology including cosmic microwave background (CMB), galaxy, and gravitational-wave (GW) direct detection. My interests include rigorous theoretical predictions, developing new analysis techniques, and real data analysis to reveal physical laws that govern the origin and evolution of the Universe. I am actively working in ongoing and future next decade CMB experiments and galaxy surveys, such as BICEP/Keck Array, LiteBIRD, ACT, Simons Observatory, CMB-S4 and Subaru.
